'Como las aves' is a video-poem / generative poem created and reflected in/from Oaxaca, establishing a dialogue with Montreal, as it was part of the exhibition 'El Barrio, Tu Barrio' project in association with Latinarte, Nuestro Flow Lab, La Maison des Amériques, Mois Latino MTL and the MEM 'Centre des mémoires montréalaises'. The curatorial work was undertaken by the artist María Hoyos.
